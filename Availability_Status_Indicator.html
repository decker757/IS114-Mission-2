Availability Status Indicator

$35.00

The Availability Status Indicator is placed outside the workspace, showing "Do not disturb" during the 25-minute countdown and switching to "Available!" during the 5-minute break. This helps to maximise productivity by minimising distractions, enabling users to focus on their tasks. This helps improve concentration, manage distractions, and maintain a consistent work pace, making it easier to tackle tasks without feeling overwhelmed. By scheduling regular breaks, it also prevents burnout and keeps the mind fresh throughout the day. The Availability Status Indicator is an add-on for the P-Watch and is activated through P-Watch's radio signals.
