Eye Care Monitor

$35.00

The Eye Care Monitor sits on a table, measuring room light intensity. A cross (✖️) appears on the LED when the light is too dim, and a tick (✔️) when it's sufficient. Pressing both buttons A and B shows the light level percentage and plays a sound. This device helps to maintain appropriate lighting conditions helps reduce eye strain, fatigue, discomfort and distraction, enabling users to focus and sustain productivity throughout their work sessions. The Eye Care Monitor is activated using the P-Watch.
