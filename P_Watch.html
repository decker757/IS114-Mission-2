Productivity Watch

$40.00

The Productivity Watch, worn on the wrist, starts a 25-minute countdown when buttons A and B are pressed at the same time. All 25 LEDs will be lit indicating the start of the work cycle. Each LED represents a minute of work. With each minute passed, 1 LED would dim. When the 25 minutes is up, the micro:bit plays a melody and alternates between heart icons celebrating the end of the work cycle and indicating the start of a break. After which, a 5-minute break timer starts, with each row of LEDs representing a minute, imitating the visual effect of a recharging battery.

The watch helps you stay focused and productive by using the scientifically proven Pomodoro Technique, breaking your work into manageable intervals with regular breaks to prevent burnout.
