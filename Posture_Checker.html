Posture Checker

$20.00

The Posture Checker is attached to the user’s shirt over his or her thoracic spine. It uses the micro:bit’s accelerometer to monitor forward tilt. If the tilt exceeds 35 degrees, which reflects the normal curvature of the spine, hunching is detected and the micro:bit produces beeps until the user stops hunching.

This promotes a better spinal health by providing real-time feedback to maintain correct posture, reducing the risk of back, neck, and shoulder pain. It prevents long-term health issues like muscle strain and improves circulation, boosting energy, focus, and productivity. By reducing physical fatigue and discomfort, it encourages healthy movement and ergonomics
