The idea behind the ELEVATE ecosystem

Our micro:bit-based ecosystem is designed to help users build healthy habits during productive work sessions in their home office. The Productivity Watch, inspired by Francesco Cirilloâ€™s Pomodoro Technique, encourages focused 25-minute work intervals followed by 5-minute breaks, promoting sustained productivity and mental refreshment. The Eye Care Monitor helps users maintain proper lighting to prevent eye strain, while the Availability Status Indicator ensures uninterrupted work sessions which maximize our productivity. The Posture Checker alerts users when they slouch, encouraging better posture. The natural curve of the thoracic spine is usually between 20 to 35 degrees from vertical, thus the threshold is set at 35 degrees forward. Together, these micro:bits work to enhance both productivity and wellness, aiming to improve posture and overall work habits over time.
